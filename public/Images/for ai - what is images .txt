LawGPT – Icon & Logo Color + Content Summary
Global color theme (applies to all icons and logo)
Background color:
Deep navy blue (very dark blue, not pure black)
Used to reduce eye strain and give a calm, legal feel.
Primary icon color:
Muted light blue (soft, low-saturation blue)
Used for symbols like scale, chat, mic, plane.
Chosen to avoid luxury or aggressive feel.
Secondary accent (where used):
Slightly brighter soft cyan / light blue
Used only for small details to represent AI or interaction.
Text color (logo only):
Off-white (not pure white)
Used to reduce glare and improve readability.
Overall theme meaning:
Calm justice, fairness, accessibility, AI support.
No gold, no red, no green, no neon colors.
